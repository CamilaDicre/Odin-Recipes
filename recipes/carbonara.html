Carbonara

Home

Description

Warm, cheesy, and creamy, Carbonara is simple yet one of the best pasta dishes you will ever have.

Ingredients

  • Spaggethi
  • Eggs
  • Guanciale
  • Black pepper
  • Pecorino Romano cheese

Steps

  1. Cook your pasta of choice.
  2. Prepare guanciale or pancetta until golden crisp.
  3. Whisk eggs and cheese together.
  4. Mix the pasta, guanciale and egg mixture in a pan on low heat.
  5. Serve and enjoy!

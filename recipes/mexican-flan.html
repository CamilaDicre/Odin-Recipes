Description

If you have a sweet tooth this is the recipe for you. Easy to make and eat, enjoyed with a rich caramel sauce.

Ingredients

  • Condensed milk
  • Eggs
  • Evaporated milk
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Mix condensed milk, egg yolks, evaporated milk together. Set aside for now.
  2. Heat the sugar to create the caramel sauce, be careful not to burn it.
  3. On a bowl of choice, pour the caramel sauce, then the condensed milk mixture.
  4. Wrap the opening with aluminum foil.
  5. Double boil the bowl.
  6. Take out and let it cool in the fridge.
  7. Serve and enjoy!

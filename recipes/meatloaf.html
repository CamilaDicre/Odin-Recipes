Meatloaf

Home

Description

Rich, and packed with plenty of protein, with just a few servings of this meatloaf you will be full for the rest of the day.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Milk
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Mix ground beef, eggs, onions, milk, breadcrumbs, salt to your taste, and pour on a loaf pan.
  2. Cook the loaf on 350 degrees F until it is no longer pink in the center.
  3. Let it cool down.
  4. Optional sauce: mix ketchup, brown sugar and barbecue sauce. Pour on top of the loaf.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
